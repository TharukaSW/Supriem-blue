## How to Match a Purchase Invoice üîÑ

**Why Match?**
Verify that supplier's invoice matches your system invoice

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Find Invoice to Match**:
   - Go to Purchase Invoices tab
   - Look for "PENDING" badge (orange)
   - OR open any unmatched invoice detail page

2. **Click "Match Invoice"**:
   - Dialog opens

3. **Enter Vendor Invoice Details**:
   - **Vendor Invoice Number**: The invoice# from supplier
   - **Vendor Invoice Date**: Date on supplier invoice
   - **Vendor Total**: Total amount on supplier invoice

4. **Real-Time Matching**:
   - System compares your total vs vendor total
   - **Green Badge**: ‚úÖ Matched! (amounts match)
   - **Red Badge**: ‚ùå Mismatched (shows difference)

5. **Save**:
   - Click "Save Match" button
   - Status updates automatically

### Match Status Meanings:

- **MATCHED** (Green): Perfect match! Amounts match within LKR 0.01
- **MISMATCHED** (Red): Difference found - investigate discrepancy
- **PENDING** (Orange): Not yet matched - action needed
